🧾 Tax & service charge MVP
PPN/VAT and service charge rates shown on PDF receipts and folio totals
Indonesia standard hotel rates: PPN 11% + Service charge 10% = 21% total ("++"). Tax applies to accommodation and all folio charges unless a charge type is marked as tax-exempt.
Tax & service charge rates

%

%
Applied before PPN. Standard hotel rate: 10%.
Combined rate
Auto 21.1% total ("++" rate)
Service charge + PPN on service charge. Shown on receipts as "++".
How rates are entered in the system
Tax-exclusive (net rates)
Nightly rates in rate plans are net (before tax). Tax added automatically on folio and receipt.
Tax-inclusive (gross rates)
Nightly rates already include all tax. No tax added on folio. Receipt shows tax breakdown from gross.
